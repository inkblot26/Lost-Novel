Wildborn

The Wildborn

The Wildborn are monstrous creatures born from nature's untamed Echo energy. Unlike ordinary animals, they possess an overwhelming instinct to hunt and consume humans. While they will prey upon other creatures when necessary, humans remain their preferred prey for reasons that are still unknown. Some scholars speculate that the Echoes within humans nourish or attract them, but no theory has ever been proven. Though many resemble beasts found in nature, Wildborn are far stronger, far more resilient, and far more aggressive, making them one of humanity's greatest threats.
Scholars generally classify Wildborn into two broad categories: Feral Wildborn and Ascendant Wildborn

Feral Wildborn

The most common type of Wildborn. Ferals are driven entirely by instinct, living only to hunt, feed, and defend their territory. They cannot reason or communicate beyond animal behavior.
Rather than wielding supernatural abilities, Ferals rely solely on their evolved physical traits. Many possess immense strength, hardened hides, razor-sharp claws, venom, powerful jaws, wings, or other natural adaptations that make them deadly predators. Their bodies alone are their greatest weapons.

Apelang

Description: The Apelang is a long-limbed primate Wildborn with shaggy brown fur mottled by patches of mossy green. Its powerful prehensile tail rivals its arms in strength, allowing it to swing through the canopy, climb sheer trunks, or wield crude objects with remarkable dexterity. Bright yellow eyes constantly dart between the branches, reflecting its restless curiosity. Its oversized hands and feet end in long, hooked fingers that cling effortlessly to bark. Entire troops fill the forest canopy with shrill cries before descending upon intruders in chaotic waves.

Babipeg

Description: The Babipeg is a massive boar-like Wildborn that stands roughly as tall as an adult human at the shoulder. Its broad, muscular body is covered in coarse dark-brown bristles and a hide thick enough to withstand ordinary blades. Four enormous tusks dominate its massive head—two projecting forward beneath its snout while another pair curve backward along its cheeks like ivory hooks. Small blood-red eyes sit beneath a heavy brow, giving it a perpetually hostile glare. Though slow-moving under normal circumstances, its thick neck, powerful shoulders, and stocky frame allow it to bulldoze through dense forests and smash aside almost any obstacle in its path. A lone Babipeg is already a formidable threat, but when several gather and charge together, their stampede becomes a devastating force capable of trampling everything in its way.

Bloomfang

Description: Bloomfangs are lean, wolf-like Wildborn with dark emerald fur threaded by living vines that weave naturally across their shoulders, backs, and tails. Small leaves and patches of moss often grow along these vines, allowing the creatures to blend seamlessly into dense forests. Their sharp amber eyes, keen sense of smell, elongated fangs, and hooked claws are perfectly adapted for tracking and bringing down prey much larger than themselves. Moving with remarkable speed and coordination, Bloomfangs hunt in instinctive packs, surrounding their prey before striking with swift, synchronized attacks.

Crashstripe

Description: Crashstripes are large cow-like Wildborn with broad, barrel-shaped bodies supported by thick, muscular legs. Their coarse hides are patterned with bold black-and-white stripes broken by ridges of hardened, calloused skin that provide natural protection against blows. They have massive heads with wide muzzles, reinforced skulls, and a pair of thick, forward-curving horns capable of delivering devastating charges. Their broad shoulders and powerful necks allow them to push through dense undergrowth and knock aside obstacles with ease. Despite their herbivore-like appearance, Crashstripes are highly territorial and will aggressively charge anything that enters their grazing grounds. Their meat is prized for its rich flavor, making them a common target for hunters despite the danger they pose.

Crawler

Description: The Crawler is a colossal spider-like Wildborn standing nearly eight meters tall. Its enormous body is covered in coarse blue-black bristles that give it a rugged, weathered appearance, while its heavily armored exoskeleton bears deep ridges and overlapping plates hardened through years of growth. Unlike true spiders, Crawlers possess twenty long, pillar-like legs, each ending in massive hooked claws capable of gripping stone and climbing sheer cliffs despite the creature's immense weight. Multiple glossy black eyes cluster across the front of its head, providing a wide field of vision, while powerful mandibles large enough to seize an adult human protrude beneath them. Though slow-moving, every step of a Crawler carries tremendous force, capable of crushing trees, collapsing walls, or flattening a small house beneath its weight. Unlike smaller spider-like Wildborn, Crawlers produce no silk or webs, relying entirely on overwhelming size and brute strength to hunt and defend themselves.

Driftmouse

Description: The Driftmouse is a fist-sized rodent-like Wildborn with an elongated body built for squeezing through narrow cracks and dense roots. Its dark brown fur is streaked with black, helping it disappear among leaf litter and fallen branches. Oversized whiskers extend far beyond its snout, constantly twitching to detect vibrations in the air and ground. Its tiny black eyes are poorly developed, but its oversized ears and sensitive whiskers allow it to navigate almost flawlessly without sight. Long clawed toes let it cling effortlessly to bark, stone, and even ceilings.

Scorchbeak

Description: The Scorchbeak is a towering vulture-like Wildborn draped in ragged black feathers stained crimson along the edges, as though perpetually singed by fire. Its bald head is covered in thick charcoal-gray hide, while its heavy hooked beak bears a scorched orange hue from years of exposure to its own corrosive secretions. Thin wisps of smoke often curl from its nostrils after feeding, giving it an eerie appearance. Broad, powerful wings allow it to circle high above the landscape for hours before diving upon weakened prey, while its long, heavily curved talons effortlessly seize struggling victims.
Hidden within its throat are specialized alchemical glands that produce a volatile acidic fluid. As the liquid is expelled through its beak, it ignites upon contact with the air, forming a stream of burning acid capable of melting flesh, armor, and even stone. Scorchbeaks rarely hunt alone, instead gathering in small flocks known as ashes, where they overwhelm prey through relentless aerial assaults and coordinated dives.

Sekteri

Description: The Sekteri is a medium-sized predatory Wildborn, roughly the size of a large eagle, with a lean body built for speed and agility. It combines the broad wings and hooked beak of an eagle with the long, slender legs of a crane, giving it a distinctive silhouette both in flight and on the ground. Its plumage blends creamy white with rich earth-brown feathers that provide natural camouflage among cliffs and forest canopies, while its vivid orange face serves as a striking warning to potential threats. The outer flight feathers along its wings are naturally hardened into razor-like blades, allowing each sweeping strike to cut through flesh with ease. Despite being capable hunters, Sekteri are light enough to perch on a tamer's shoulder or forearm, making them valued companions for scouting, hunting, and messenger duties. On the hunt, they patiently observe from above before diving with remarkable precision to strike unsuspecting prey.

Skareth

Description: Skareth is a colossal eagle-like Wildborn roughly the size of a large house, its broad wings capable of casting a sweeping shadow across the ground as it soars overhead. Its wings are layered with metallic feathers edged like forged blades, shimmering in shades of silver and storm gray beneath the light. A long, muscular neck supports a fearsome head that combines the hooked beak of an eagle with the angular jaws of a wyvern. Jagged bone horns sweep backward from its skull, giving it a regal yet intimidating silhouette. Its piercing golden eyes remain fixed on prey from incredible distances, while its enormous talons curve like polished scythes, powerful enough to split stone and tear through thick armor with a single strike. Skareth nests atop the towering cliffs of the Verdant Ring, where it is often seen gliding silently above the endless mists of the Unknown East.

Sunquill

Description: The Sunquill is a large avian Wildborn found throughout the deserts and highlands, with a wingspan broad enough to carry a fully grown rider through the skies. Its sleek body is covered in brilliant golden-yellow feathers streaked with white, giving it the appearance of sunlight sweeping across the dunes. Powerful wings provide swift, graceful flight, while its long tail feathers act as natural rudders, allowing precise turns and stable gliding even in strong desert winds. Its long legs end in sharp talons suited for perching atop cliffs and rocky outcrops, and its narrow hooked beak is strong enough to pierce hide and crack bone when threatened. Naturally alert and even-tempered, the Sunquill prefers to flee rather than fight, making it a favored aerial mount for scouts, messengers, and skyrunners.

Thorndevil

Description: The Thorndevil is a squat, desert-dwelling lizard-like Wildborn covered from head to tail in jagged, horn-like spines. Its rough scales are mottled in sandy brown, rust-red, and pale yellow, allowing it to blend almost perfectly with rocky dunes and sunbaked cliffs. Rather than a visible mouth, it possesses narrow moisture-absorbing slits concealed between the armored scales along its body, drawing water directly from rain, fog, or damp sand. Rows of detachable thorns line its back, flanks, and tail, which it can launch with surprising speed and accuracy before they gradually regrow over time.

Tremorhoof

Description: The Tremorhoof is an enormous horse-like Wildborn standing nearly eight feet at the shoulder. Its body is heavily built with dense muscles, a broad chest, and pillar-like legs resembling living tree trunks. Thick slate-gray fur covers its frame, while rugged plates of hardened bone naturally reinforce its shoulders, chest, and forehead. Its massive, broad hooves are deeply cracked and worn from traversing rocky terrain, each step leaving deep impressions in the earth. A thick mane runs from the top of its neck down its back, while its powerful tail helps maintain balance across steep and uneven ground. Despite its imposing appearance, the Tremorhoof is remarkably calm, intelligent, and extraordinarily resilient. Once bonded through the Echo of Tame, it becomes one of humanity's most dependable beasts of burden, capable of hauling immense loads across terrain where ordinary horses would quickly falter.

Webling

Description: Weblings are hand-sized arboreal Wildborn resembling small spiders, each with a bulbous charcoal-black body marked by pale white stripes across its abdomen. Eight long, wiry legs twitch constantly beneath their bodies, allowing them to move silently across branches and their own webs without disturbing a single thread. Clusters of tiny reflective eyes cover the front of their heads, faintly glimmering in the darkness and creating the unsettling illusion that the forest itself is watching intruders. Though individually fragile and easily crushed, Weblings gather in vast colonies capable of blanketing entire stretches of forest with dense, adhesive webs that ensnare prey far larger than themselves.

Wox

Description: The Wox is a lean, fox-like Wildborn built entirely for speed. Its long legs, narrow chest, and flexible spine allow it to sprint with terrifying acceleration across forests and open plains alike. Sleek orange fur fades into pale cream along its underside, while black markings accent its ears, paws, and bushy tail. Its narrow muzzle is lined with rows of needle-like teeth designed for tearing flesh rather than crushing bone. Rarely seen alone, Wox hunt in disciplined packs, relentlessly pursuing their prey until exhaustion leaves it defenseless.

Ascendant Wildborn

Ascendants are a more evolved class of Wildborn distinguished by intelligence and the ability to wield extraordinary powers. While fewer in number than Ferals, they are encountered often enough to be recognized as a major threat throughout the world.
Unlike Ferals, Ascendants can think strategically, communicate, coordinate attacks, set traps, and exploit human weaknesses. In addition to their physical prowess, they possess unique supernatural abilities similar to humanity's Echoes, allowing them to manipulate elements, create powerful techniques, or develop abilities beyond the limits of ordinary beasts.
Because of both their intelligence and powers, Ascendants are considered among the most dangerous enemies humanity can face.

Alpha Wox

Description: The Alpha Wox is the evolved leader of a Wox pack, standing nearly shoulder-high to an adult human. Its body is taller, leaner, and more muscular than that of ordinary Wox, with long powerful legs built for explosive bursts of speed. Its orange coat fades into silver-white along its chest, tail, and underbelly, while streaks of pale gray run through its fur like flowing currents of wind. Long ears and a sweeping tail constantly sway as though caught in an invisible breeze, even on windless days.
Unlike ordinary Wox, an Alpha Wox commands the wind itself. It can propel itself through the air in rapid lunges, unleash cutting gusts from its howl, and overwhelm prey through unmatched speed and battlefield control. Lesser Wox instinctively follow its commands without hesitation.

Blinkclaw

Description:The Blinkclaw is a feline-sized Ascendant resembling a sleek black panther cub, though far leaner and unnaturally graceful. Its short midnight-black fur is broken by faint smoky-brown streaks that seem to shift whenever it moves. Long limbs, oversized paws, and an unusually flexible spine allow it to crouch impossibly low before springing forward. Its glowing amber eyes never blink, silently tracking every movement with unsettling precision, while long ivory claws remain partially exposed even at rest.
Rather than relying on brute strength, Blinkclaws hunt through sudden bursts of displacement, disappearing and reappearing a few meters away in the blink of an eye. They often circle prey unseen before delivering a lethal strike to exposed flesh or weak points in armor.

Duskfang

Description: The Duskfang is a colossal cobra-like Ascendant whose muscular body can exceed six meters in length. Its charcoal-black scales shimmer with deep violet hues, while pale gray markings spread across its expanded hood like drifting smoke. Thick ivory fangs extend well beyond its lower jaw, easily piercing armor and bone alike. Narrow emerald eyes watch with eerie patience, rarely blinking as they wait for prey to wander within striking distance.
Beyond its deadly venom, specialized glands within its throat produce a dense toxic vapor that pours from its mouth in slow-moving clouds. Victims caught within the poisonous haze quickly lose their vision, struggle to breathe, and succumb to paralysis long before the Duskfang delivers the final bite.

Glimstep

Description: The Glimstep is a graceful deer-like Ascendant standing just over a meter at the shoulder. Its slender body is covered in short silver-white fur dusted with pale blue, giving it the appearance of moonlight upon fresh snow. Thin branching antlers curve elegantly above its head, framing a brilliant white crystal suspended naturally between them. Gentle blue eyes and delicate features give the creature an almost peaceful appearance despite its dangerous nature.
The crystal between its antlers stores condensed Echo energy that can be released as devastating blasts of light. When threatened, the Glimstep repeatedly blinks across short distances in flashes of white radiance, making it nearly impossible to pin down. It often toys with intruders by appearing and disappearing among the trees before unleashing concentrated beams of energy from unexpected angles.

Mimivane

Description: The Mimivane is a tall, chameleon-like Ascendant standing nearly two meters when fully upright. Its slender body is covered in fine iridescent scales that constantly shift in color, allowing it to blend seamlessly into almost any environment. A long muscular tail aids its balance, while elongated fingers end in hooked claws capable of climbing sheer surfaces with ease. Its independently rotating eyes observe different directions at once, though they remain unnervingly motionless whenever the creature assumes another form.
Unlike ordinary camouflage, the Mimivane can reshape its body to imitate animals—or even humans—with remarkable accuracy. Only subtle imperfections betray the disguise, such as its motionless eyes, slightly elongated limbs, or unusually stiff movements. When exposed, it abandons its disguise entirely, revealing its towering reptilian form before attacking with blinding speed, powerful claws, and a whip-like tongue.
