The Wildborn

The Wildborn are monstrous creatures born from nature's untamed Echo energy. Unlike ordinary animals, they possess an overwhelming instinct to hunt and consume humans. While they will prey upon other creatures when necessary, humans remain their preferred prey for reasons that are still unknown. Some scholars speculate that the Echoes within humans nourish or attract them, but no theory has ever been proven. Though many resemble beasts found in nature, Wildborn are far stronger, far more resilient, and far more aggressive, making them one of humanity's greatest threats.
Scholars generally classify Wildborn into two broad categories: Feral Wildborn and Ascendant Wildborn

Feral Wildborn

The most common type of Wildborn. Ferals are driven entirely by instinct, living only to hunt, feed, and defend their territory. They cannot reason or communicate beyond animal behavior.
Rather than wielding supernatural abilities, Ferals rely solely on their evolved physical traits. Many possess immense strength, hardened hides, razor-sharp claws, venom, powerful jaws, wings, or other natural adaptations that make them deadly predators. Their bodies alone are their greatest weapons.

Skareth

Description: Skareth is a colossal eagle-like Wildborn roughly the size of a large house, its broad wings capable of casting a sweeping shadow across the ground as it soars overhead. Its wings are layered with metallic feathers edged like forged blades, shimmering in shades of silver and storm gray beneath the light. A long, muscular neck supports a fearsome head that combines the hooked beak of an eagle with the angular jaws of a wyvern. Jagged bone horns sweep backward from its skull, giving it a regal yet intimidating silhouette. Its piercing golden eyes remain fixed on prey from incredible distances, while its enormous talons curve like polished scythes, powerful enough to split stone and tear through thick armor with a single strike. Skareth nests atop the towering cliffs of the Verdant Ring, where it is often seen gliding silently above the endless mists of the Unknown East.
