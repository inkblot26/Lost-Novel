Lost: Ashes of Vengeance

Verdant Echoes

Verdant Echoes are physical manifestations of the Earth’s lingering will—living tomes infused with natural energy. These tomes grant abilities when absorbed, after which they crumble into dust, imprinting their power into the user’s memory. Bound in living bark and inscribed with glowing ink, Echoes appear mysteriously upon the defeat of monsters, or may occasionally be found growing within nature itself. Most individuals can absorb only two to three Echoes, depending on their physical strength and compatibility. Verdant Echoes are broadly classified into two categories: Root Echoes and Rare Echoes. ECHO USER RANKING SYSTEM 1. Echo User – A basic wielder of Echo abilities. 2. Echo Master – A highly skilled individual with refined Echo control. 3. Echo Weaver – A transcendent wielder whose Echo abilities have evolved beyond conventional limits. CLASSIFICATION OF ECHOES Humanity classifies Echoes into seven major categories: a. Elemental Echoes Echoes derived from the fundamental forces of nature, governing heat, water, earth, air, lightning, and other elemental energy states. These Echoes allow direct manipulation of environmental phenomena and elemental reactions. • Echo of Earth – Grants control over earth, stone, sand, mud, and mineral terrain through direct manipulation of natural materials. • Echo of Fire – Grants the ability to generate and manipulate flames and heat through Echo energy. • Echo of Lightning – Grants control over electrical currents and lightning through the generation and discharge of charged Echo energy. • Echo of Water – Grants control over water in both liquid and frozen states through fluid manipulation and temperature regulation. • Echo of Wind – Grants control over air currents, atmospheric pressure, and wind flow through manipulation of moving air. • Echo of Magma (Rare) – Grants control over molten rock and volcanic heat through extreme temperature manipulation. • Echo of Metal (Rare) – Grants control over metallic substances and refined ores through reshaping and reinforcement. • Echo of Tremor (Rare) – Grants the ability to generate vibrations and seismic shockwaves through force transmission. b. Nature Echoes Echoes drawn from living systems, organic growth, biological adaptation, regeneration, and natural decay. These govern the cycle of life and deterioration. • Echo of Bloom – Grants accelerated plant growth and botanical manipulation through Echo-infused vegetation and spores. • Echo of Glimmer – Grants the ability to generate and manipulate bioluminescent light through natural Echo energy. • Echo of Heal – Accelerates natural recovery and biological restoration through restorative Echo energy. • Echo of Rot (Rare) – Accelerates decay and decomposition in organic matter through corrosive natural Echo energy. c. Beast Echoes Echoes born from primal instinct, biological mutation, and the adaptive traits of beasts and Wildborn creatures. These Echoes manifest physical enhancements, transformations, and instinct-driven abilities. • Echo of Beast – Temporarily manifests beast-like traits through partial bodily transformation. • Echo of Metal Skin – Transforms flesh into metallic, armor-like plating reinforced by Echo energy. • Echo of Pact – Creates and commands translucent beast-like companions formed from Echo energy. • Echo of Tame – Allows bonding with Wildborn creatures and influencing their behavior and cooperation. • Echo of Flesh (Rare) – Grants unstable control over one’s biological structure through mutation. • Echo of White Wing (Rare) – Manifests radiant white wings formed from concentrated Echo energy. d. Senses & Awareness Echoes Echoes focused on perception, awareness, sensory enhancement, stealth, and spatial interpretation beyond normal human limits. • Echo of Perception – Enhances sensory awareness beyond normal human capability. • Echo of Silence – Dampens or nullifies sound through Echo energy control. • Echo of Strength – Continuously enhances physical power through reinforced Echo circulation. • Echo of Voice – Enhances vocal resonance, tone, and sound projection. • Echo of Camouflage (Rare) – Allows visual blending with surroundings through light and color manipulation. e. Mind & Soul Echoes Echoes that interact with consciousness, memory, cognition, emotion, fate, and spiritual perception. • Echo of Psyche – Allows perception of and subtle influence over thoughts and emotions. • Echo of Recall – Grants perfect memory retention and retrieval of all experiences. • Echo of Fate (Rare) – Causes unpredictable shifts of fortune or misfortune that influence surrounding events. f. Space & Motion Echoes Echoes governing movement, gravity, spatial displacement, and momentum. • Echo of Grasp – Exerts invisible force to pull, push, or manipulate objects at a distance. • Echo of Path – Marks routes for instant traversal or retracing. • Echo of Pocket – Creates a small internal spatial storage space. • Echo of Gravity (Rare) – Manipulates gravitational force and spatial pressure. g. Knowledge & Creation Echoes Echoes that enable formation, replication, manipulation, and conceptual creation of objects and energy constructs. • Echo of Barrier – Generates protective energy structures. • Echo of Shape – Manipulates and reshapes physical materials using Echo energy. • Echo of Weapons – Manifests translucent weapons formed from Echo energy. • Echo of Blood (Rare) – Allows controlled manipulation of blood as a physical medium. • Echo of Duplicate (Rare) – Creates temporary replicas of physical objects using Echo energy. • Echo of Ink (Rare) – Brings drawings, symbols, and markings into temporary physical existence.
