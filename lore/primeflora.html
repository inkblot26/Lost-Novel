Primeflora

The Primeflora

Primeflora are extraordinary plants that have adapted to the harsh and ever-changing wilderness, evolving far beyond ordinary vegetation. Through generations of survival, they have developed remarkable traits—some store vast reserves of water within swollen trunks, others produce fruits with rare medicinal or alchemical properties, while a few react to movement or changes in their surroundings like silent guardians of the wild. Their wood, roots, bark, leaves, flowers, sap, and fruits are highly prized for the creation of echotech, medicines, and other valuable materials, making Primeflora among nature's most sought-after resources.

List of Known Primeflora

Baobao Tree

The Baobao Tree is a colossal Primeflora with a massively swollen barrel-shaped trunk that can span the width of a small house. Its smooth, pale gray bark is stretched tightly over the trunk, broken only by shallow wrinkles that resemble weathered stone. Near its crown, thick, stubby branches twist outward like giant roots reaching toward the sky, giving the tree the unmistakable appearance of having been planted upside down. Sparse clusters of rounded gray-green leaves grow only at the tips of its branches. Hanging beneath them are large oval Bao Fruits—hard, slate-gray pods that contain naturally filtered drinking water. The trunk itself stores enormous water reserves, and when cut, clear water pours from the wound before the bark slowly seals itself over time.

Duskveil Fern

The Duskveil Fern is a low-growing Primeflora with broad, layered fronds colored deep violet fading into black near their edges. Its leaves possess a soft velvety texture that seems to swallow surrounding light. After sunset, the fern releases clouds of microscopic black spores that drift silently through the air, absorbing nearby sound and creating unnaturally quiet pockets of wilderness. Entire forests carpeted with Duskveil Ferns become eerily silent after nightfall. Its spores are harvested to create stealth echotech and sound-dampening cloaks.

Nightveil Rose

The Nightveil Rose is a rare Primeflora found only upon the mist-shrouded slopes of Mount Tharros. Its slender dark stems support elegant blossoms whose velvety petals are so black they appear to absorb the surrounding light. Silvery droplets of dew cling to each bloom throughout the night, making them appear as though scattered with stars. Once picked, the flower never wilts, preserving its perfect form indefinitely. However, if replanted beyond Mount Tharros, its petals gradually fade into a deep crimson, as though separated from the mountain that gave them life. Its blossoms are prized for perfumes, rituals, ceremonial offerings, and shadow-based echotech.

Sacred White Tree

The Sacred White Tree is an immense Primeflora found only atop the isolated cliffs of the Lipad Clan. Every part of the tree—from its smooth trunk and twisting branches to its leaves—is a brilliant ivory white that shimmers softly beneath sunlight and moonlight alike. Its translucent leaves resemble layered feathers, gently reflecting the light around them. Only on rare occasions does it bear luminous white fruit, and on even rarer occasions, one of those fruits conceals a Verdant Echo that grants the Rare Echo of Wing.

Walking Rhiza

The Walking Rhiza is a wandering Primeflora whose thick trunk resembles an ancient palm weathered by centuries of travel. Instead of remaining rooted in place, dozens of long, rope-like roots descend from its base, spreading like the legs of a colossal spider. These roots slowly lift, bend, and step across the landscape, allowing the tree to roam in search of fertile soil or hidden water. Its crown bears broad emerald leaves that sway even without wind, while its bark is dark brown and deeply grooved from constant movement. At night, friction between its moving roots and the earth produces a faint amber glow, causing migrating groves to resemble floating rivers of fire weaving through the wilderness. The dense yet flexible roots are highly prized for crafting Flicks echotech.
