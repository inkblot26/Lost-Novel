Synopsis

In the age of the Galactic Awakening, humanity reached beyond the stars. Hopeful and curious, they encountered the Chaturi, a solitary traveler of boundless intellect and mystery, unlike anything ever known in a vessel called Oumuamua. Its arrival on Earth brought awe and unity. For a time, there was peace. Knowledge flowed freely, and wonder bloomed.

But peace is fragile. As fear and ambition took root, nations turned on one another, each seeking to control the Chaturi. Diplomacy gave way to deception. War followed. Disillusioned by humanity’s hunger for power, the Chaturi vanished without a trace or a word. Some say it left in disappointment. Others believe it glimpsed something beyond human understanding.

Then the world convulsed. Monstrous beasts roamed the land. Storms poisoned the skies. Oceans swallowed continents. Forests reclaimed cities. Technology, from satellites to weapons to digital memory, ceased to function, wiped away by forces unknown. Civilization crumbled. Billions perished. And yet, in the ruins, survivors awakened to something new, or perhaps something ancient — Verdant Echoes, a power drawn not from machines, but from life itself. Centuries have passed. Now begins the Verdant Age.
